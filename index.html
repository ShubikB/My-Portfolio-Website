More About Me

As a lifelong tech enthusiast, I've always been fascinated by the possibilities of technology. From tinkering with my grandfather's radio as a child to exploring the latest advancements today, I've dedicated my life to understanding how technology can positively impact the world.

Driven by my passion, I enrolled in the Bachelor of Information Technology program at Kent Institute Australia in Sydney. Over the past two years, I have honed my skills in programming languages like Java, HTML, CSS, PHP, and SQL.

My ultimate goal is to create innovative solutions that transform lives and redefine how we interact with technology.
